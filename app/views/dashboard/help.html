3 Attribution Campaign Setup

Tell me about campaigns!

To create an Attribution Campaign, click the New Campaign button on the dashboard page and select Attribution Campaign. Only a campaign name is needed.

To install the campaign: Note the Registration ID for the campaign. Simply add that ID as a query parameter (p0c) in your campaign clickthrough (i.e. Adroll Landing URL). An example format for the url is as follows: www.getpixeltag.com?p0c=55c6aeabb25bb50300000001

4 Retargeting Campaign Setup

Tell me about campaigns!

To create a Retargeting Campaign, click the New Campaign button on the dashboard page and select Retargeting Campaign. Enter a name, and valid landing page URL.

Note: The campaign will be pending initially as it takes up to 48 hours for campaign details to be synced with the Ad Networks. Click "Enable" to activate the campaign within Salesforce.com.

To navigate to your campaigns, simply click "Campaigns" in the menu and select an existing campaign from the list.

From here you can:

  • Edit the campaign name and Landing URL
  • Adjust the associated creative assets
  • Enable/Disable the Campaign

Tell me about creatives!

Campaigns may be associated with multiple creative assets. To upload a new creative, navigate to the menu bar and select “New Creative”.

Your creative file size must be under 150k and in .PNG, .JPEG, or .GIF format.

The dimensions of your creatives should fit one of the following:

  • Leader board Banner: 728x90 pixels
  • Medium Rectangle: 300x250 pixels
  • Skyscraper Banner: 160x600 pixels

Simple Guidelines:

  • All content must obey geographic laws for: Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Gambling.
  • All content in reference to Religion, Politics, Dating, Sexual Health, and the Military must be positive and non-controversial.
  • Images may not contain adult content, offensive language, or be misleading.

A Note on Landing URLs!

Entering a valid Landing URL is important. Your landing URL must:

  • Clearly disclose the company or person responsible for the ad and landing page
  • Not use any third parties’ brand, logo or design without the express, written permission of such party
  • Contain relevant information pertaining to the good or service being promoted by the ad (and not some other good or service intended to bait-and-switch a consumer)

5 Data Sync

In order to generate individual URL's for your leads/contacts, each record will need to be edited, as the update is dynamic. This can easily be accomplished by Mass Updating an empty checkbox on the Lead/Contact object. Your administrator should be able to assist with this need.

But my Admin isn't available!!

Not to worry!! You can still deploy Retargeting Campaigns to your prospects using the pxl_switch__PixelTags__c field on either Leads or Contacts. Campaign conversions will still be recorded and can be reconciled later.
